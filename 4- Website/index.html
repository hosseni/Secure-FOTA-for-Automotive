01

ARM Cortex M3 MCU

The STM32 Blue Pill is a popular development board based on the STM32F103C8T6 microcontroller. It offers a cost-effective solution for embedded projects with its 32-bit ARM Cortex-M3 core, running at up to 72 MHz. The board features 64KB of Flash memory, 20KB of RAM, and various communication interfaces, including UART, SPI, and I2C.

02

Touch Screen Dash Board

The Nextion HMI version NX8048T070 is a 7.0-inch intelligent display module designed for Human-Machine Interface applications. Featuring a resistive touchscreen with 800x480 resolution, it offers a clear and vibrant graphical interface. The NX8048T070 is powered by the Nextion Editor, which allows developers to create custom GUIs effortlessly using drag-and-drop elements.

03

Distance sensor for cpllesion detection

An ultrasonic sensor is a non-contact distance measurement device that uses ultrasonic waves to determine the distance between the sensor and an object. It works by transmitting sound waves and measuring the time it takes for the waves to bounce back after hitting the object

04

RGB Leds Indicators

An RGB LED combines red, green, and blue light sources in one package. Using Pulse Width Modulation (PWM), the LED can vary the intensity of each color, enabling a wide range of colors and dynamic lighting effects in applications like mood lighting and displays.

05

MP3 player Module

The DFPlayer Mini is a compact and versatile MP3 player module designed for easy integration into projects. With a built-in microSD card slot, it can play audio files in formats like MP3 and WAV. It supports UART communication, making it ideal for DIY audio applications, including voice prompts, music players, and sound effects in robotics and IoT projects.

06

Analog Potintumeter

A potentiometer, commonly known as a "pot," is an adjustable three-terminal resistor used to control electrical resistance manually. It has a rotating or sliding control that varies the resistance, allowing users to adjust voltage levels, audio volume, and other parameters. Potentiometers are prevalent in electronics, robotics, and audio applications.

07

LCD Display Screen

A SPI TFT LCD (Serial Peripheral Interface Thin-Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display) is a compact and versatile display technology. It employs the SPI protocol to communicate with microcontrollers, making it ideal for embedded systems and IoT devices. SPI TFT LCDs offer vibrant colors, fast response times, and are widely used in handheld devices and wearable gadgets

The ESP32 Wroom is a powerful and popular Wi-Fi and Bluetooth-enabled microcontroller module. Developed by Espressif Systems, it combines a dual-core processor, ample memory, and various interfaces for seamless connectivity. The ESP32 Wroom is widely adopted in IoT projects, home automation, and prototyping due to its reliability and extensive capabilities
